BORN

 hero prabhas(born in 23 oct 1979 ,chennai,tamil nadu,india) he is a popular indian actor
            He made his film debut with the 2002 drama film, Eshwar. His works include Varsham (2004), Chatrapathi (2005), Chakram (2005), Billa (2009), Mr. Perfect (2011), and Mirchi (2013). In 2014, he made his Bollywood debut with Action Jackson. In 2015, he played a titular role in the epic Baahubali: The Beginning. The sequel of this movie is set to release in 2017.

HERO PRABHAS CAREER PATH

    One of the highest-paid and highest-grossing actor in Indian cinema. Prabhas is hailed as the "First Pan-Indian Superstar". Prabhas was born in a Telugu family to late U. Suryanarayana Raju and Siva Kumari. His family hails from Mogalthur, Andhra Pradesh. He is the youngest of three children and is the nephew of Telugu actor Uppalapati Krishnam Raju.
